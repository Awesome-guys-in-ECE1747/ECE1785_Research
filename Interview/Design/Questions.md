# Question Designed

## Targets

1. Get the emotional factors in several projects the participant previously get involved in.
2. Evaluate the potential influence factors of such emotions qualitatively.
3. Assess the outcome of the corresponding projects.

---

4. Analyze the relationship between potential influence factors, emotional factors and outcomes.
5. Compare with the result/conclusion of quantitative study.
6. Draw conclusion in general.

## Factors

### Predicted influence factors

- Restricted time
- High quality requirement
- Other social factors (e.g. annoying teammates, complex & time-costing troubleshootings, unfixable issues)

### Predicted emotional factors

- joy
- fear
- sadness
- disgust
- anger

### Predicted outcomes

- High efficiency
- High quality

## Questions

- Tell me some memorable/considerable projects you've done previously.
- Is there a due date of your project at the time?
- What's your personal target of the project?
- Tell me more about the procedure of the project? Is there anything interesting/signifcant that makes it memorable?
- How's your emotional situation throughout the way?
- May I know your exact completeness in the project of your aim, due time or something else?

## Notes

In order to take fully consideration of participant's condition, the interview will be done with local languages of the participants.

The award for each participant will be the balances/gift card in online shopping or gaming platform of $10.

The interview will be audio recorded and related materials (p.s. especially for opensource developers) will be attached together and convert into script individually.
